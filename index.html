About Me

My name is Bobby Smith and I am a Software Developer at IBM Design. Before working in software development I got to do some fun and exciting things. I attended Columbia College in Chicago, IL and received a bachelor's degree in Acoustics. After college I worked at Hinge Recording Studio in Chicago, IL as an assistant audio engineer and got to work with amazing artists like Kayne West, Lupe Fiasco, Donald Lawrence, and Kurt Elling. I've also had the pleasure to make my entrepreneurial self happy. I have opened and ran a coffee shop, a bar, and a contracting company for a few years. I have been a DJ for 15+ years going from being a hobby to touring. You can still see me DJ the occasional event. DJing led me to be a record fanatic and I have built a pretty decent record collection that is always being added to. Also, I know the lyrics to more 90's hip-hop songs than a normal person should, but one day that knowledge will come in handy.

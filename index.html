About

My name is Bobby Smith and I am new to this big wonderful world of web development. I'm a recent graduate of MakerSquare in Austin, TX, an intensive 12-week development boot camp. I learned a lot of things about web development but above all I have learned how to think like a web developer. I'm excited to continue learning more about design, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript frameworks, and all the intricate ways clients interact with servers.

Before web development I have did some fun and amazing things. After college I worked at a recording studio in Chicago, IL as an assistant audio engineer and got to work with artists like Kayne West, Lupe Fiasco, Donald Lawrence, and Kurt Eling. I've also had the pleasure to make my entrepreneurial self happy. I opened and ran a coffee shop, a bar, and a contracting company. I have been a DJ for 15+ years going from hobby to touring, although now it's more of a hobby. DJing led me to being a record fanatic and building quite a large record collection that is always being added to. Also, I know the lyrics to more 90's hip-hop songs than a normal person should, but one day that knowledge will come in handy.
